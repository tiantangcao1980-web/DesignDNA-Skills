Method 01

Proven rhythm. Unhurried.

25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes of rest — repeat four times, then a longer break. The Pomodoro rhythm has helped people ship thoughtful work since 1987. FocusTimer keeps the rhythm and strips everything else.

Method 02

One decision per session.

Every start prompts you to name the single most important thing for this block. Finish or don't — but when you sit down, you know what you're working on.
